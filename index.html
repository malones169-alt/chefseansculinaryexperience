The Chef Behind the Food

A Lifelong Love of Real, Honest Cooking

Chef Sean is an Ann Arbor native with over two decades in the culinary world — from the kitchens of Zingerman's Roadhouse and The Black Pearl, to crafting unforgettable events for the University of Michigan and HOMES Brewery.

His approach is simple: exceptional ingredients, scratch techniques, and menus tailored to you. Whether it's an intimate five-course dinner or a large-scale catered event, every plate is made with intention and heart.
